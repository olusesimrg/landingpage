I am Damilola Aleje

"I’m fondly called (Makanaki). I am a TV Presenter || Radio & Podcast Presenter || Script Writer, Content Provider and sometimes a producer."

Damilola Aleje is a versatile broadcaster and TV host she currently is a presenter with Nigeria’s Foremost Television Station- Nigerian Television Authority (NTA); she hosts Live entertainment shows and Red Carpet events.

With Confidence poised with professional friendly presentation style, she is never at loss for words; there is never an awkward phrasing or dead air in her presentations.

Before joining NTA, she was Deputy Editor the renowned Nigerian Safari having worked with several online news agencies.

She also a voice-over artist owning credits to several major brands adverts and documentaries; a Content provider, and a Script writer. She worked as a Co-host for a Midnight show on Nig info Lagos. Her rare expertise was recently featured in a just-concluded Lagos @50 project.

Outside the television scene, Damilola Aleje is a Fashion Designer. She owns a certificate in of Fashion Designing from a United Nations Agency. Currently Damilola Serves as an Independent Host and Red- Carpet Presenter for different Platforms.
